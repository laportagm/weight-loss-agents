How the numbers were sourced

Source trust tiers

Every efficacy figure above traces to Tier 1 or Tier 2. Consumer aggregators (GoodRx, Healthline) and peptide-vendor / SEO blogs were deliberately excluded as sources for any number — they were where the earlier draft's inflated orforglipron and "data-maturing" MariTide figures came from.
One caveat on comparison: trials differ in length (46–104 weeks) and in which estimand they headline (efficacy / per-protocol vs. intent-to-treat), so cross-trial numbers are directional, not head-to-head. Tolerability tags summarize discontinuation and GI data qualitatively. This is an educational reference, not medical advice — these figures come from supervised trials with slow dose titration, and that supervision matters most for the harder-to-tolerate agents.
